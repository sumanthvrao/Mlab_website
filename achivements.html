Recent Works

Reinforcement Learning

AI Bot that can play games better that humans.

Created an Artificially Intelligent Bot that can play games better than a human, improves continuously over several iterations in a virtual environment and finally integrated it to any general game environment through reinforcement learning, of a particular genre.

Vrchitect

Making it easy to visualize floor plans in a VR environment.

Making it easy to visualize floor plans in a VR environment with editing capabilities as well. With our app, you can visualize your house only when the architectural plan is ready so you know how it would look after the construction. You can add furniture, move them around to get the feel of the house as well.

Forget Me Not

Android app to help memory disabled patients

Forget me not is an android app made by interns in the summer internship 2018 for people suffering with Dementia and alzheimer's. The app includes features such as facial recognition, guided assistance to home, a schedule reminder, interactive brain development games , a buitl-in calling feature and much more. An article on the app was published few days after it was completed and presented at the roadshow.

Energy Aware Low Latency Routing in WSNs

Wireless Sensor Networks

We have designed an energy aware low latency routing algorithm for encrypted data in Wireless Sensor Networks. The encrption algorithm used is Elliptical Curve Cryptography. The routing algorithm ensures improved lifetime and reduced latency thus optimizing the network itself.

Indra

Generation of Gait on humanoid robots using machine learning techniques

Indra is a humanoid Platform that can be used by other developers to customize the bot to do specific tasks. One such instance is being implemented as part of Indra 2.0. Indra 2.0 is used to train the bot to mimic the walking action. We use Genetic algorithms to generate the gait values and later improve the values through Re-inforcement Learning.

GAN

Catch Me If you GAN! - conditional synthesis of human faces from textual descriptions using Generative Adversarial Networks.

Mapping of Textual Description to Facial Image : Developed a program that can understand various features of an image of a face and classify the various features and store them in a database. The pre-processed data is referred during information extraction, when the user enters a text describing a person, which will return the image of the person from the database. This software can be used to depict the work of a sketch artist, and can lead to a more accurate criminal investigation.

Ball Catching Bot

A Bot that can catch a ball thrown anywhere withing its reach.

A Bot that can track the trajectory of the ball thrown and the coordinates where it would reach the maximum height. With this information the ball catching bot orients itself to catch the ball that is thrown in a certain radius.

Oculus

Controlling electronic devices through eye movement

Oculus is the project that enables users to control electronic devices through eye movement. This is done through collecting EEG signals and training the collected data to predict the eye movements.

Explodroid

The labelled SLAM bot : Explordroid

Explordroid is a labelled SLAM bot that can map a room and also answer queries regarding different items present in the room. Its where robotics meets Machine Learning to create an intelligent bot that could also be used for security purposes in places like warehouses.

Makin Waves

Motor Imagery using EEG signals

Prior to performing a Motor Action, certain patterns of EEG signals are generated in the Brain. This project tries to find a specific pattern in the signals that can allow us to determine what Motor Action a volunteer is on the verge of performing (Right Hand, Left Hand, Feet, Tongue), without any actual movements from the volunteer.
